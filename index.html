WELCOME TO THE IKON’S UNIVERSE.

So, what’s an Ikon?

Ikons are fantastic robots that can store vast amounts of knowledge. They can fly, activate shields, and shoot lasers, but, the most interesting feature is the light they emancipate, much like the light of the sun, it influences everything, and everyone.

It is said that the color of their light fuses with the color of the aura with whom they bind with.

TYPES OF IKONS

Rammers are known for their defensive abilities. From Auras that grant more natural armor, to evasive feats, these Ikons prefer to deal damage by using their overwhelming constitution. Built with harder materials than the latter, these Ikons will ram into their opponents leaving them in disarray.

Acceleration Armor Constitution Energy

Dischargers are known for their lasers. These Ikons prefer ranged battle and are quite unpredictable, as they can use their energy effectively with their power. Mix between hard and light materials.

Acceleration Armor Constitution Energy

Shrouders are known for their Auras. These Ikons prefer to boost their power through energy. Usually built with light materials, they rely on their magical abilities to counter enemy Ikons.
